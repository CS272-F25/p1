Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Global Kitchen collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Information We Collect

Global Kitchen does not collect personal information unless you choose to submit it through our contact form. Basic usage data (such as browser type or pages visited) may be collected automatically through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you submit voluntarily is used solely for responding to your inquiries. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

Local Storage & Saved Recipes

Global Kitchen uses your browser’s localStorage to save recipes you mark as favorites. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us or any external service.

Third-Party APIs

Our website retrieves recipe data from external APIs. These services may have their own privacy policies regarding data handling. Global Kitchen does not control or manage how these third-party services operate.

Cookies

Global Kitchen does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Any stored data exists solely to support website functionality.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you submit a message through our contact form, you allow us to use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page.
